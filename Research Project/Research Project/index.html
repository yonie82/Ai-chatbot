About CUT

The Central University of Technology (CUT), located in the heart of South Africa, is a dynamic institution dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and producing high-quality graduates ready to meet the challenges of the 21st century. Since its establishment in 1981, CUT has become a hub for cutting-edge research, technology, and forward-thinking education.

At CUT, we nurture a deep passion for technology and research, equipping our students with the practical skills and innovative mindset needed to thrive in fields such as Engineering, Health Sciences, Information Technology, Humanities, and Management Sciences. Our commitment to career-focused education ensures that our graduates not only excel academically but are also fully prepared for the demands of the workplace.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant academic community, we foster a culture of creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving. Through work-integrated learning and strong industry partnerships, our students gain hands-on experience, positioning them as leaders and innovators in their chosen fields.

At CUT, we believe in transforming lives through education, research, and innovation, producing graduates who make a meaningful impact on their industries, communities, and the world.
